How to Add Your Demo Videos

  1. Upload your videos to YouTube, Vimeo, or your preferred video hosting platform
  2. Get the embed URL:
    • YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID
    • Vimeo: https://player.vimeo.com/video/YOUR_VIDEO_ID
  3. Update the component: Open src/app/components/gallery/gallery.component.ts and replace YOUR_*_VIDEO_URL_HERE with your actual video URLs
  4. Uncomment the iframe: In src/app/components/gallery/gallery.component.html, uncomment the iframe section
  5. Add safe pipe: Create a safe pipe to sanitize video URLs (see instructions in component)
